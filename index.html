Founder's Vision

Immortalized was founded by Joe Micallef as a new kind of creative platform: part talent agency, part marketing agency, part production studio. Built in Los Angeles and informed by a background in animation, multimedia, visual storytelling, and emerging technology, the studio is designed to move at the speed of culture.

Joe Micallef is a creative technologist, director, educator, and multidisciplinary visual strategist with a background spanning animation, production, design, storytelling, and emerging media. Through Immortalized, he brings together talent, aesthetics, and technology to create brand experiences that feel fashion-led, future-facing, and culturally sharp.

"His vision for Immortalized is rooted in a belief that the strongest creative work does more than communicate. It leaves a mark."
